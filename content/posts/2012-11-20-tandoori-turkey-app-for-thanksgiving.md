![](/images/2012/11/sshot1.png)"TandooriTurkey' is an app available from google playstore for Android phones/tablets.

The app is a six Thanksgiving recipes package with recipes for tandoori turkey, baked potatoes, buttered peas, roasted vegetables, apple crumble and kalakand for an Indian sign off to a tastebud tantalizing ,easy prep meal .

The a la carta meal with fast food prep times can be downloaded for just $0.99
