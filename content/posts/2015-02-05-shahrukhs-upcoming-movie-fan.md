Shahrukh Khan is going to be playing the role of stalker again similar to Darr. The movie is to be released on August of this year, and Shahrukh Khan talks of this movie as one of the most challenging projects.

Maneesh Sharma, who won the Filmfare for his directorial debut in Band Baaja Baarat is the promising director of Fan.

SRK fans have a lot to look forward to this double role starrer movie and SRK in bad boy’s role again.

[SRK previously described the idea behind “Fan” saying](http://www.fashionnstyle.com/articles/44827/20150204/shahrukh-khan-new-movie-fan-has-him-playing-a-threatening-stalker-details-released.htm), “I liked the story of ‘Fan’ and it’s not like what has been mentioned on Twitter. I have never seen life from a point of someone who likes me. It would be very interesting for me to be on the other side. To be a fan, what does it mean felt like a good idea and it’s diametrically opposite to who I am so it would be very interesting.”
