[![](/images/2011/08/rihanna-300x168.jpg)](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/rihanna.jpg)Rihanna's envious red tresses come at a hefty price tag, as it was revealed that she spends a whopping $23,000 per month to get her hair to that perfect shade.

The 23-year-old Barbadian beauty has enlisted the help of celebrity stylist Ursula Stephen, who charges $3,300 per day to keep her looking great. With her several public appearances each week, RiRi pays a small fortune to be styled to perfection.
