[![](/images/2011/09/AIC.gif)](http://www.artinstituteofchicago.org/)Family Festival: Celebrate Diwali!

Join us on Saturday, October 29, between 10:30 am - 3 pm for a free Family Festival to celebrate Diwali, the Hindu festival of lights! Create a workk of art inspired by Indian traditions at activity stations throughout the Ryan Education Center, including the Little Studio for our youngest visitors (ages 3-5). help make a great paper rangoli, on a studio floor. Make a colorful costume to wear as you dance and play! Enjoy dance workshops led by Kalapriya Dance at 11 am, 1 pm and 2 pm.

 

**Hours:**

10:30 - 3 pm

**Location:**

Art Institute of Chicago - Ryan Education Center

111 S. Michigan Ave.

Chicago, IL 60603
