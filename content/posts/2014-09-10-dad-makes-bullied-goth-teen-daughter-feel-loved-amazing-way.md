Back to school is stressful, for some kids it could be a nightmare , the fear of getting teased because they don't fit in , insensitive teachers because they don't follow instructions well-the list is a mile long. A supporting friend, kind words from a teacher or a parent that runs exta to make his kid feel okay is all it takes to bring the mile back . Bullying is a serious issue, it alters the personality of the child forever.

[A new viral ad for German home improvement brand Hornbach](http://time.com/3303918/goth-teen-dad-hornbach-ad/), serves as a good reminder that even if a child prefers all black, wears a leather collar and avoids team sports, they still deserve a good, loving support system. If that means painting your home black, so be it.
