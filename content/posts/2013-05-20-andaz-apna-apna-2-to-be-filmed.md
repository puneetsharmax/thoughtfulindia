![andaz-apna-apna](/images/2013/05/andaz-apna-apna.jpg)Hopefully AAA 2 will have the same cast as the first one – however if the humor is as good as the first one the star cast might not matter. Or maybe it could be a *ghazani* look for Aamir and *dabangg* look for Salman -it surely will rock then .

 

Andaz Apna Apna had not done well at the box office –it caught on only when people watched it on televisions –and then it never lost its popularity.

 

[It has taken nineteen years to get a sequel for this one. Raj Kumar Santoshi will be directing it again.](http://www.indiatimes.com/entertainment/bollywood/aamir-salman-in-andaz-apna-apna-2_-78619.html)

 

AAA 1 is memorable because of the lovely, nonsensical dialogues – it was hard not to laugh.
