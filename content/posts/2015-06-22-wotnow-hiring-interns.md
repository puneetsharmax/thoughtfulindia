**Attention: Naperville Residents.**

 

[WotNow your local social calendar app](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/2015/05/wotnow-a-free-app-developed-by-naperville-residents/) that helps manage life events better by synchronizing all your calendars (Facebook, Google, Phone etc.) into one calendar is hiring interns.

 

Sign-up for WotNow app summer internships

Sign-up for marketing internships - learn valuable communication & marketing skills, win gift cards based on performance, and add community hours/summer internship to resume and college apps.
Skills Needed - Outgoing, good communicators who are tech-savvy and responsible.
About Internship - The internship involves "manning" the info desks at the three Naperville libraries (and soon park district location), explaining the WotNow app to the visitors, and answering their questions. Interns can pick convenient hours at the choice of their library.

The minimum time commitment is five hours a week.

 

For more details please contact Vidhya Iyer @ **vidhya22@gmail.com**
