**6) ****Micro Life on Earth and STEM**


Do you know Life on earth is just not all what we see with our eyes? Students will explore this world of tiny things which are not visible to our naked eyes but make up the life on earth. How does light work? Do you know why plants turn pale and tall without light? Students will explore the STEAM of this tiny world from atoms to molecules to circuits to cells to this world of life. Get to do hands-on experiments of culturing bacteria, find out how plants control their growth.  Learn Newton’s laws of motion in real life and understand the chemistry of matter and its changes.

**8)** **Doctor in Training**


This course is geared towards kids who are interested in pursuing a career in medical sciences in future. They will be introduced to human anatomy, skeletal system, parts of the nervous system, five senses (smell, sight, taste, vision, hearing), heart and stomach and how they function. They will be engaging in practical activities such as hearing and taking pulses, examining noses and ears. The children will also be taught to read the basics of reading an x – ray. Students will also perform a heart’s (sheep) dissection and explore the science behind the blood flow and a lot more which will ultimately culminate into designing and engineering an artificial heart.
