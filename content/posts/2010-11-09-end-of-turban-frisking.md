 An Indian lawmaker from Punjab said Tuesday she had asked President Barack Obama to put a stop to the U.S. border practice of frisking Sikh turbans.

 Sikhs worldwide have long protested the American security measure as discriminatory and unnecessary in a world with machines for body scanning and metal detection.[read more](http://www.google.com/hostednews/canadianpress/article/ALeqM5jXGme51tg425CrFeqeTLh2qKP2zQ?docId=5079175)
