Madhuri Dixit got served with a legal notice for endorsing Maggi Oat Noodles as a healthy breakfast choice.

 

The ad shows Madhuri getting the whole family moving and then serving them Maggi Noodles! Maggi Noodles the most convenient food, is now relabeled as the silent killer. It contains MSG, lead and zinc in excessive quantities.

 

Actors endorse anything and everything for money. Madhuri just got unlucky.
