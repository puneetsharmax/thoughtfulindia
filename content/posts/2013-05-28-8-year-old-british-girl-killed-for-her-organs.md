This British girl was on her first ever-foreign trip to India.  This now is the last trip of her lifetime.

 

The eight-year-old girl was visiting her grandparents in Punjab, India.  She was admitted in a hospital for dehydration. Instead of treating her for dehydration the hospital workers injected her with a liquid that caused her to become very sick eventually resulting in her death.

 

[The parents are saying –that when they received her body in Britain –all her internal organs were missing apart from her eyes.](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/crime/10055772/British-schoolgirl-murdered-for-her-organs-in-India-family-claim.html)

 

In India laws are made so that they are broken over and over again. There has been a ban against organ harvesting since 1994 –but we all know that this restriction is only paper-true.
