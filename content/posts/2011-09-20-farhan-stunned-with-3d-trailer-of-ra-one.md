**[![](/images/2011/09/farhan.jpeg)](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/farhan.jpeg)Actor-filmmaker Farhan Akhtar was bowled over when superstar **[**Shah Rukh Khan**](http://www.newkerala.com/news/2011/worldnews-71189.html#)** gave him a **[**sneak peek**](http://www.newkerala.com/news/2011/worldnews-71189.html#)** into the 3D trailer of his mega-budget project "RA.One".**



"@iamsrk showed us two songs and the trailer of 'RA.One' in 3D last night... to put it simply... Outstanding!!!!" Farhan posted on his Twitter page Tuesday.



"RA.One", a superhero film, is releasing this Diwali. Meanwhile, Farhan is busy wrapping up "Don 2: The Chase Continues", a sequel to the 2006 film, "Don: The Chase Begins Again", which itself is a remake of the 1978 film "Don".

"Last song of Don in the cans... now the film is officially complete," he added.

The film, starring Shah Rukh in the lead, is set for release in Dec 2011.
