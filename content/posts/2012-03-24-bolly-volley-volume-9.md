*A monthly shower of Bollywood previews, reviews and my unsolicited views*

*by Arup Raha*

 

Someone recently asked me which movie I am looking forward to, now that the earlier favorites for the year, Paan Singh Tomar and Kahaani, have already released. There are many but I would name just one. Matru Ki Bijli Ka Mandola. Yes, that is the name of a movie and not the sign on a highway dhaba in Haryana!
