![](/images/2012/09/brain.jpg)The Indian governments will no longer issue NORI (no obligation to return to India) certificates to medical students. The [TOI](http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Govt-makes-it-harder-for-doctors-to-stay-on-in-US/articleshow/16330652.cms) states that this is being done so that the doctors who come to USA for higher studies do not settle here.
