Arnold Schwarzenegger is in Chennai for the audio launch of Vikram-starrer Tamil romantic-thriller "I"

 

The audio launch will at Jawaharlal Nehru stadium has the megastar Rajinikanth sharing the dais with Schwarzenegger.

 

["A.R. Rahman will perform all the songs from the album live. He will also be supported by dancers, who will perform wearing the actual costumes and make-up from the film. The show will go on for about two and a half hours," the source added.](http://www.ndtv.com/article/india/arnold-schwarzenegger-and-rajinikanth-to-share-stage-in-chennai-today-591940)*

Directed by Shankar, "I" also stars Amy Jackson, Suresh Gopi and Upen Patel in important roles. It will release around Diwali.
