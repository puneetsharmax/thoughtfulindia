***

Today I miss you, you've been gone for a week


I can imagine your smile, your grin cheek to cheek


Etched in my mind are visions of your face


Moments in my memory of how you make my heart race


Your Beauty, Your Passion, the sparkle in your eyes


Your honesty, your truth, your soul never lies


I don't know why you have come but I'm glad that you did


We dance, we laugh, together like kids


We live in the moment, no expectations are there


Somehow it just works, the way that we care


Where this may go, neither one of us can see


We stay in the moments and just let them
