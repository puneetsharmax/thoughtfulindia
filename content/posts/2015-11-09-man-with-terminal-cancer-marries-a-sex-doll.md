A 28-year-old Chinese man who is diagnosed with terminal cancer wanted to experience the joys of wedding ceremony but did not want to leave a grieving widow. So he chose not to marry a human but instead marry a sex doll.

 

He got a life-size inflatable doll and made the doll look like a bride with the wedding dress and all. He even went for the first dance with the doll.

 

His wedding pictures have gone viral first in China and now are circling different parts of the world.
