## Exposed by sixth wife, Bihar conman arrested

The Rohtas police arrested a 35-year-old man from Dehri after his wife lodged a complaint alleging that he had married five more women in the last 10 years, pretending to be an Intelligence Bureau (IB) official.

Vijay Kumar Singh, an unemployed and resident of Nawada district, had been living in Dehri with his sixth wife, Poonam, whom he married in March this year.
