[Steve Martin](http://www.nydailynews.com/topics/Steve+Martin) is playing a dad again — and this time it's not a movie!

The 67 year-old "Cheaper by the Dozen" star and his wife, Anne Stringfield, 41, have welcomed their first child.

A rep for the legendary comedian confirmed to the Daily News, "Steve Martin and his wife are new parents and recently welcomed a child."

Details about the couple's bundle of joy have been kept very quiet.

Martin and Stringfield married in 2007 at his home, in a surprise ceremony for "party" guests including Tom Hanks and "Saturday Night Live" creator Lorne Michaels
