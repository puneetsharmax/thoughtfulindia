#### Parenting an overweight child can be difficult

When it comes to helping an overweight or obese child slim down, parents routinely blow it, pediatric obesity experts say. Some resort to nagging or coercion, others put the child on a restricted diet, and still others issue sweeping bans against foods containing sugar or fat
