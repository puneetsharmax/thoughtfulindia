There are no prices in this restaurant, only a suggestion to donate some money. Mosaics a restaurant in Bartow, Florida is helping to fight hunger.

The restaurant attracts all kind of people, those who have and have not. Mosaics wants to bring  people together. This "pay as you can" restaurant is popular among people from all walks of life.
