#### Special Needs Yoga Insrtuctor

We were meeting Karen in a coffee shop.

While the place was super-busy with office crowd bustling around nervously, Karen was the epitome of calm. Our conversation was easy; Karen talked about how she got involved in Yoga from an early age.

Karen was first introduced to yoga by her mum, who still practices yoga at the age of seventy four.
