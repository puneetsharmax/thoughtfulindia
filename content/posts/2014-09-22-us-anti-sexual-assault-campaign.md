The White House have launched their new ’It’s on Us’ anti sex-assault campaign aimed at encouraging young men to intervene when they witness situations that could lead to a sexual assault.

Jon Hamm, Kerry Washington, Rose Byrne, Questlove and others joined President Barack Obama and Vice President Joe Biden in support of the ‘It’s On Us’ campaign.

The new public awareness campaign, in partnership with Generation Progress, will include multiple PSAs and is supported by commitments from several organizations, companies and social media platforms, as well as about 200 campus student bodies.
