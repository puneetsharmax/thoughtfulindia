[
](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/questofgod.jpg)I have been on a lifelong quest for God, that wondrous entity which would be the solution of all my problems. I could never describe God, but it did seem that understanding God meant eternal peace, bliss and joy. However, my background really did not do much to clarify the vision of God that I felt deep within the inner core of my heart.
