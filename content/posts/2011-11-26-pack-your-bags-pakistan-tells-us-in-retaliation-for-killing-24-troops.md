Going a step ahead of merely condemning the outrageous NATO attack on a military outpost in Mohmand, the Defence Committee of Cabinet (DCC), on Sunday, decided to close with immediate effect the NATO/ISAF logistics supply lines through the country.

[caption id="attachment_4591" align="aligncenter" width="543" caption="Prime Minister Syed Yusuf Raza Gilani chairs the meeting of Defence Committee of the Cabinet at Prime Minister House on Saturday"][![](/images/2011/11/defence-committe-gilani-APP-543.jpg)](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/defence-committe-gilani-APP-543.jpg)[/caption]
