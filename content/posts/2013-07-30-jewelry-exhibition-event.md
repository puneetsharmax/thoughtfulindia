**On August 2nd & 3rd (Friday & Saturday), *Motifs Handcrafted Jewelry *is holding its summer Exhibition/Sale event. *Peacocks & Paisleys* and *Sterling* are the two collections being presented at the event.**


[![Paisley-Upl](/images/2013/07/Paisley-Upl.jpg)](https://www.thoughtfulindia.com/2013/07/jewelry-exhibition-event/paisley-upl/)


***Peacocks & Paisleys* features jewelry pieces that celebrate the resplendent colors of summer. The collection draws from classic Indian themes of the dazzling colors of the peacock's plumage, and the delicate floral elements of paisley designs.**


** ![Sterling-Upl](/images/2013/07/Sterling-Upl.jpg)**


***Sterling* is more contemporary, featuring bright metallic jewelry-pieces with intricate wire-and-bead work. This collection complements Western outfits and evening wear.**


Also available are gorgeous readymade Tibetan necklaces with large color beads and pendants. Each jewelry piece is handcrafted. You can also place order to get custom-designed jewelry to match your outfits.


Prices are attractive, and there’s no pressure to purchase !


**Where: 2804 Whitehall Lane, Naperville, IL 60564**


**When: Fri-Sat, August 2nd & 3rd****, from 11:00 am to 6:00 pm**
