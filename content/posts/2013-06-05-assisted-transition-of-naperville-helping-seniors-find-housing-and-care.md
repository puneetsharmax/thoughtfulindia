![logo](/images/2013/06/logo-300x91.jpg)Assisted Transition is an organization that helps seniors find housing and care. It is completely a free service.

All of us know how hard it is to find a place suitable for the ageing dad, or the distant aunt who is in no position to live in her big house all by herself now. Most of the times due to deteriorating health, seniors prefer living in an old age home. Old age homes provide safety, security and similar social groups –but not all old age homes are equal.

If you are at crossroads looking for an ideal home for your dad, mom, or aunt and don’t have the time to research all options –give Assisted Transition a chance. They will listen to your needs and budget and will try to find the best place for your loved senior relative.

One of the unique features of Assisted Transition is that they just don’t forward you the leads of various apartments/old age homes, they research based on your needs and then recommend you the ideal place- all this is free of charge.

They also help with finding a caregiver/nurse if your elderly relative needed to be taken care of in their own homes. Needless to say they will do the research and hard work in trying to find the ideal help –all this is again free!

Assisted Transition aka “ senior care concierges “ – one stop solution for all type of needs for seniors.
