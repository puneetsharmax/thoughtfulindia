Donald Trump is blunt and people are beginning to tolerate him a bit more. He is not shocking anymore. People are used to his ridiculous jokes, racist comments – let’s just say he does not shock us anymore.

 

He has won millions of hearts by saying that -"India is the check to Pakistan."  He calls Pakistan as the probably the most dangerous” comparing it to North Korea. Pakistan is "a serious problem" because they have nuclear weapons that work and "a lot of them” just like North Korea and its "mad man", Trump explained.

 

He explains further "You have to get India involved ... They have their own nukes and have a very powerful army. They seem to be the real check ... I think we have to deal very closely with India to deal with it (Pakistan)," said Trump, about his foreign policy goals.

 

He decided not to answer the question – Whether he would send troops to Pakistan. Instead he said "You want to have a little bit of guess work for the enemy."

 

He is still a strong frontrunner.
