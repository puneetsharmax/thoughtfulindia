All that buzz about Krrish 3 was right, the movie will roll early next year and Priyanka Chopra will be seen opposite Hrithik Roshan once again.
Though there were rumours that the actress is not being considered for the sequel, reports are on that Priyanka is finalized for the film. She will continue her character of a reporter as the movie will take forward the story where Krrish left. Roshans were toying with the idea of Krrish sequel for the last four years but nothing concrete happened. Now, as they have moved on from the debacle of Kites, making Krrish 3 is prime on their plans.
Coming to Priyanka, the actress has some big films lined up for next year: she has Saat Khoon Maaf with seven male co-stars, Don 2 with Shahrukh Khan, and also Anurag Basu
