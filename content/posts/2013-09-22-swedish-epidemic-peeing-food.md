A man in Sweden was caught [peeing on selected fruits and vegetables](http://www.thelocal.se/50364/20130921/). Shoppers in the store stared at him in disbelief when they saw him peeing on apples and oranges.

Police was called and the man was arrested. Both the people and the police have said that they have never seen such a thing. The man , it seems was intoxicated and had no clue of what he was doing.

All the fruits had to be thrown (naturally!)

 

Last year a drunken man (again!) was caught [peeing on pizza](http://www.thelocal.se/43132/20120910/) . In this case , the 29-year-old truck driver was denied beer by the restaurant staff because he was already too drunk. The driver got into a fight and put his stamp on the pizzas by urinating on them!
