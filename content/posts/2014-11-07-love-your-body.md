All of us have been there , we want to change our ears , forehead, big butt , flabby arms , thin lips …..probably grow more hair ….the list is endless. With the burgeoning influence of social media and photos being taken on every occasion -all of us want to look beautiful , look like stars of the celluloid. Think about it , beauty is what you feel inside . Let’s embrace and love ourselves , whatever form or color that we are blessed with. Watch this video by the [Jubilee Project ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f0tEcxLDDd4)on how our attitudes differ from children . Let’s awaken the child in us .
