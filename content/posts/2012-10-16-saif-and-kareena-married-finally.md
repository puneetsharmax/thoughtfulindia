Even as the hoopla over the grandest wedding in the town approaches its crescendo, Saif Ali Khan entered into matrimony with five year long girlfriend Kareena Kapoor today in Mumbai. The high profile couple of Bollywood made it official, as Saif and Kareena went for a registry marriage today with Karisma being one of the witnesses. Along with Karishma, the three primary witnesses were parents of Kareena - Randhir Kapoor and Babita and Saif's mother Sharmila Tagore.According to sources, Saif and Kareena got married between 11.30 am and 12 pm when Saif sent his car to fetch Registrar Surekha Lokhare from Bandra Family Court today. Saif, the Nawab of Pataudi entered his original name in the marriage application. Saif Ali Khan signed his papers in the name of Sajid Ali KhanSaif and Kareena after the marriage waved at the crowd as husband and wife. Saif looked dashing in a grey kurta while Kareena wore a green salwar-kurta with a heavy embellished red dupatta wrapped around her. She teamed it up with minimal jewelry, red bangles and left her hair open.
