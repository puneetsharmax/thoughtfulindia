Ekjut local theater group of the Chicago area presents Yoni ki baat/ Vaginal Monologues. These are stories of women their struggle with relationships, career, societal pressures. If you have always wanted to be part of a drama group-on stage or off stage- contact Promila Kumar: [ekjut@gmail.com](mailto:ekjut@gmail.com) /847-668-3565. Auditions are going on for actors. If you are interested in helping out with the production- there is ample opportunity.

 

**When: October 10,2015**

 

**Where: North Central College Auditorium, Naperville**

 

**Commitment: Ten rehearsals from August on Sunday afternoons.**
