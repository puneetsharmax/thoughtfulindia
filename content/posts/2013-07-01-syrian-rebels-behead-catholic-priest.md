![murad](/images/2013/07/murad.jpg)Jihadists have beheaded Franciscan Father Francois Murad a catholic priest in Syria.

 

The Vatican City has [confirmed the death](http://www.catholic.org/international/international_story.php?id=51537) of the priest. The 49-year-old priest was mercilessly killed in Gassanieh, in northern Syria. It is being said that Murad was killed by the jihadi group Jabhat-Al-Nusra. This is an al-Qaeda led group, which claimed [responsibility to Damascus bombing](http://www.albawaba.com/news/nusra-claims-responsibility-damascus-bombing-502098) last Tuesday.
