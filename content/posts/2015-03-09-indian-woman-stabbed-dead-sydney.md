A woman who died in a stabbing attack in a Sydney park was speaking to her husband in India on the phone at the time of the attack on Saturday evening, reports say.


Prabha Arun Kumar was caught on a CCTV camera shortly before the attack.![](https://s.yimg.com/ea/img/-/150308/inline_westmead_murdwe_1afnuga-1afnugg.jpg?x=656&sig=lCurwK2QKKshDdzaT9nGzw--)

Australian police have appealed for help from the public, and said there was nothing to suggest that the attack was racially motivated.
