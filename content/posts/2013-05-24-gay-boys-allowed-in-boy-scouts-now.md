![boy-scouts-3](/images/2013/05/boy-scouts-3.jpg)Boy Scouts of America has voted to allow openly gay boys into its troupes. It took [more than a hundred years](http://www.suntimes.com/news/metro/20308261-418/boy-scouts-vote-to-allow-gay-scouts.html) to lift this ban.

 

It has been not easy to fight this ban. Conservative parents and churches are of the view that this is [not “morally right”](http://www.nytimes.com/2013/05/24/us/boy-scouts-to-admit-openly-gay-youths-as-members.html?_r=0). There has been a talk going on among conservative parents to open an alternative organization like Boy Scouts. It could be called  “[character development organization for boys,”](http://www.nytimes.com/2013/05/24/us/boy-scouts-to-admit-openly-gay-youths-as-members.html?_r=0)

 

The ban is still on for gay adults. They can’t still join Boy Scouts.

 

This definitely is a step up towards world becoming more liberal.
