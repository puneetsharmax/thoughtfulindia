![](/images/2012/09/wa_no-homework_silke_preview.jpg)Middle Schools in Tennessee will no longer grade homework or give extra credit . Homework is for understanding the concepts and not for grading . What this means is if a student does not do well in the homework , he will have to redo it and this will not impact his grades.
