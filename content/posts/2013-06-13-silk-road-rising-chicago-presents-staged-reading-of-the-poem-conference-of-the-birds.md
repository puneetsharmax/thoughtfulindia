Award winning Chicago­based theatre company, Silk Road Rising, is producing a stagedreading of Conference of the Birds, an adaptation of the mystic poem by 12th century Sufi poet, Farid ud Din Attar. The poem is adapted by Meena Natarajan and will be directed by Dipankar Mukherjee.
