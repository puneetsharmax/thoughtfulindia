The 'surprise' birthday party of actor Hrithik Roshan, who turned 39 today, was no surprise to him, thanks to the media.

A few papers said that Hrithik's wife Suzanne had organised a surprise yacht party for her hubby last night.

"People got to know about it and even I got to know about the party through papers. When I asked Suzanne about it she denied it... But we had fun at the party," Hrithik told reporters today.

As fans and mediapersons gathered outside his residence in suburban Juhu, Hrithik insisted he never expected to feel so special on his birthday.

"Every birthday I don't expect to feel so special...

since my last film Agneepath released a year back, I thought this birthday won't be so special to everyone. I did not know I was still so important."

His next film, 'Krrish 3', is expected to release in Diwali this year.

Often praised as the India Greek god, the actor said there is no mantra to looking young. "When someone tells you... you look younger I think they actually mean older.

Frankly there is no mantra to stay younger... one must feel young at heart."
