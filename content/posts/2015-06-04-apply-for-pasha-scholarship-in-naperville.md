[
PASHA Orthodontics](http://www.pashaorthodontics.com/home) invites high school seniors to apply for their scholarship.

**Details
**
1st Place: $450
2nd Place: $350
3rd Place: $250

1. Please write a bio about yourself describing you, your interests, and your strengths.
2. Please describe your five-year and ten- year career goals (provide as much detail as possible).

*Applicants must be entering college in Fall 2015
*Applicants must be former or current patients.
*Prize money must be used toward college expenses.
***Send application to pashaorthodontics@gmail.com by Aug 1st. **
